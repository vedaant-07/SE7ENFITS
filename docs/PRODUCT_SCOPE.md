# SE7ENFITS Product Scope

SE7ENFITS is an AI-powered gym ecosystem for Indian gym members and gym owners.

## Product split

1. SE7ENFITS mobile app for members and gym owners
2. Gym owner web dashboard for full laptop/tablet management
3. SE7ENFITS admin web dashboard for internal team only
4. Shared backend API

Members and gym owners can both use the mobile app. Gym owners also get the same owner dashboard on web. Admin control is website only.

## Member app

Core modules:

- Google login
- Member onboarding
- Profile setup: name, DOB, gender, height, weight, location, fitness goal, activity level, experience level
- Gym code linking
- Free limited plan after gym invite
- Subscription plans
- Daily workouts
- Weekly workout planner
- Exercise library
- Animated workout demos and videos
- Workout tracking: sets, reps, weight, rest, calories, PRs
- Food scan calorie detector for premium users
- Manual calorie calculator
- AI trainer for gym/workout/nutrition questions only
- Gym section showing linked gym, machines and trainers
- Challenges
- Reward points
- Leaderboards for premium users
- Progress tracking
- Notifications

## Free member invite workflow

1. Gym owner adds a member phone number from owner app or owner web dashboard.
2. SE7ENFITS sends SMS or WhatsApp invite to the member.
3. Message says: You unlocked AI Virtual Trainer with the gym name and gym code.
4. Member opens link and installs app from Play Store or App Store.
5. Member signs in with Google and joins using the gym code.
6. Member gets limited free access.
7. Member can upgrade to unlock full AI, workout, nutrition, leaderboard and premium features.

## Main business workflow

1. Gym owner creates gym profile.
2. Gym owner receives a unique gym code.
3. Gym owner adds or invites gym members by phone from app or web.
4. Member receives SMS or WhatsApp invite.
5. Member installs SE7ENFITS and signs in with Google.
6. Member joins with gym code and receives limited free access.
7. Member upgrades to a paid plan.
8. Member gets linked to that gym.
9. Gym owner earns commission on successful subscription.
10. Member workouts are generated using that gym's available machines.

## Unique value

SE7ENFITS AI trainer creates workouts based on the exact machines available in the user's linked gym. Gym owners can invite members directly, activate limited free value, and convert them to paid plans with commission tracking.
